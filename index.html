About Me

Hey there! I'm a machine learning researcher with a passion for using technology to make a positive impact on people's lives. Most recently, I worked at Kintsugi, an early stage startup, where I developed a machine learning model that could diagnose depression from speech. It was an incredibly fulfilling experience, and I'm excited to see where this technology will go in the future.

I hold both a Master of Science in Computer Science and a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science and Mathematics from UC Berkeley. Throughout my career, I've developed a diverse set of skills in machine learning, spanning various domains. I have extensive experience in natural language processing, speech processing, and computer vision. I'm constantly looking for ways to expand my knowledge and stay on top of the latest advancements in the field. At the moment, I'm actively seeking a full-time position, but I'm also open to exploring contractor opportunities. I am currently located in San Francisco.

Beyond my professional pursuits, I'm an avid adventurer who enjoys outdoor activities like backpacking, hiking, and kayaking. When I'm not working or exploring the great outdoors, I spend my downtime at home with my girlfriend and our two cats, Epsilon and Delta. I also enjoy baking and reading, with a particular passion for epic fantasy. If you have any favorite series you'd like to share, I'd love to hear them!
